Hire the right builder for you

Before you make a final decision about who to hire, make sure that you're completely happy with your choice of tradesman. Try to choose a builder who you feel you can trust and with whom you'll be comfortable communicating throughout your project.

Ask if the builder's work is guaranteed

Good builders might guarantee materials and workmanship. Ask if guarantees are insurance-backed in case the builder goes out of business. If you choose or buy materials then they are normally your responsibility

Check trade association membership

Does the builder belong to a professional body or trade association? Contact those organisations to verify membership and enquire about what assurances they offer to consumers if things go wrong.
